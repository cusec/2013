What is CUSEC?

The Canadian University Software Engineering Conference is a three-day event that brings together undergraduate and post-graduate students for learning, networking, and sharing their passion for software. CUSEC 2013 will happen from January 17-19, 2013 in Montreal, Quebec at the Delta Centre-Ville.
